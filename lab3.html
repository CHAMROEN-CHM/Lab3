Decrete Math

Discrete math is the backbone of computer science, dealing with stuff
like logic, sets, graphs, and algorithms to solve problems step by step
instead of using continuous values.

Statistics

Statistics is the branch of math that focuses on collecting,
analyzing, interpreting, and presenting data to make sense of
patterns, trends, and uncertainty.

Linear algebra

Linear algebra is a branch of mathematics that studies vectors,
matrices, and linear transformations, and it is usually taught as a
subject separate from discrete mathematics.
